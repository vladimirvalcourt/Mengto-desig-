01 / The Sanctuary

"Eight can sleep here but the mountain feels infinite."

Cedar Lodges is not a hotel — it is an act of reverence toward the Galiyat forest. Built from cedar and mountain stone, it sits at the heart of Pakistan's most storied highland belt.

Surrounded by Ayubia National Park, ancient trails, and the kind of silence that cities have forgotten. A world entirely apart, yet only 58 miles from Islamabad.

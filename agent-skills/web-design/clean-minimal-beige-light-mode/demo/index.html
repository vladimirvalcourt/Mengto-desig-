FAQ

Questions people usually ask before they start

How do sessions work? +

You choose a mentor, book a time, and meet online for a 1:1 session focused on your goals, questions, or portfolio.

How much does it cost? +

Prices vary by mentor, experience, and session format. You can compare profiles and choose an option that fits your budget.

How do I choose a mentor? +

Use filters like field, language, experience, and price. Then review profiles, ratings, and focus areas to find the right match.

Can I become a mentor without teaching experience? +

Yes. If you have strong practical experience and can guide others clearly and respectfully, you can apply to become a mentor.
